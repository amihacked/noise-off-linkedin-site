# 🔇 NoiseOff for LinkedIn

**Filter your LinkedIn feed by language. Hide promoted posts. Take back control.**

NoiseOff is a free browser extension for Chrome and Firefox that filters
your LinkedIn feed in real time — hiding content you don't want to see
while keeping everything you do.

---

## Features

### 🌍 Language Filter
Set your preferred languages and NoiseOff hides posts written in other
languages. Uses your browser's built-in language detection — no data
sent anywhere.

### 🚫 Hide Promoted Posts
Automatically detects and hides sponsored and promoted posts that
LinkedIn disguises as organic content.

### 🖼️ Hide Media Content
Optionally hides images, videos, and link previews from filtered posts
for a cleaner feed.

### 👁️ Show Anyway
Every hidden post shows a collapse bar with a "Show anyway →" button.
One click reveals the post. Your choice is respected for the session.

### ⚡ Master Toggle
Turn all filters on or off instantly with a single switch.

## Privacy

NoiseOff is built with privacy as a core principle:

- ✅ No data collection of any kind
- ✅ No network requests — never phones home
- ✅ No tracking, analytics, or telemetry
- ✅ No access to your LinkedIn account
- ✅ Language detection runs entirely in your browser
- ✅ Settings stored locally in your browser only
